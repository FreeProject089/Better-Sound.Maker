# 🚀 What's New in v1.0.3?
**Keeping your Mods up-to-date and your workflow smooth!**

## 🌟 Community Sync 2.0
- **One-Click Updates:** You've downloaded a preset from the community and the author updated it? A yellow **"Update Available"** button will now appear automatically on your preset! Click it to get the latest sounds and settings instantly.
- **Real-Time Refresh:** No more waiting for GitHub's cache. Use the new **Refresh** button in the Community panel to see new presets the second they are uploaded.
- **Smarter Import:** We fixed the issues where clicking the "Install" button sometimes did nothing. It's now smoother and faster than ever!

## 🛠️ Advanced Preset Edition
- **Update your Presets:** Made changes to your project? Instead of making a new "Mod v2", you can now click the **Edit** icon on your existing preset and choose **"Replace with current selection"**. It updates your preset perfectly and keeps your list clean!
- **Version Control:** When you export a preset to share, you can now set your **Version Number** and **Update Number** right before saving. This helps the community know when you've released something new.

## ✍️ Bulletproof SDEF Editor
- **Raw Mode Power:** Love editing the code directly? Your custom lines and comments are now **preserved** even if you switch back to the Visual mode. The editor is now non-destructive!
- **Persistent Changes:** Switching between different files while editing? Your unsaved changes are now kept in memory until you hit Save or exit the editor. No more losing your hard work.

## ⚡ Performance & Polish
- **Faster Library Loading:** We've improved how the app scans your files. It's more stable and provides better feedback while building your folders.
- **Dev Panel Update:** The developer tools are now more discreet. You can reopen them anytime with the small gear icon ⚙️ at the bottom.
- **General Fixes:** Countless small bugs found by the community have been squashed!
