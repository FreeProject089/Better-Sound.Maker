[CENTER][SIZE=6][B]🚀 Better ModMaker v1.0.3 - The Dynamic Sync Update[/B][/SIZE]
[SIZE=4][I]Your essential companion for DCS Sound Modding just got smarter![/I][/SIZE][/CENTER]

[HR][/HR]

[B]Hello DCS Community![/B]

We are excited to announce the release of [B]Better ModMaker v1.0.3[/B]. This update focuses on making the community experience "Dynamic" — ensuring you always have the latest sounds and settings without the manual hassle.

[SIZE=5][COLOR=#FFD700]🌟 WHAT'S NEW?[/COLOR][/SIZE]

[B]1. Dynamic Community Sync[/B] 🔄
The app now talks directly to the [URL='https://github.com/BetterDCS/Better-Sound.Maker-Community-Presets']Community Repository[/URL].
[LIST]
[*] [B]Auto-Update Detection:[/B] If a preset you've installed (like the F/A-18C Hornet or C-130) gets an update from the author, a yellow [COLOR=#FFD700][Update Available][/COLOR] button will instantly appear on your card.
[*] [B]Real-Time Refresh:[/B] Use the new "Refresh" button to bypass server caches and see the latest community uploads the second they go live.
[/LIST]

[B]2. Smarter Workflow & Export[/B] 🛠️
[LIST]
[*] [B]Update Existing Presets:[/B] Instead of cluttering your list with "Mod v2", "Mod v3", you can now edit an existing preset and choose "Replace with current selection". It updates everything and increments your internal version automatically!
[*] [B]Pre-Export Check:[/B] You can now set your [B]Version[/B] and [B]Update Number[/B] right before exporting, ensuring your followers know exactly what's new.
[/LIST]

[B]3. Bulletproof SDEF Editor[/B] ✍️
[LIST]
[*] [B]Non-Destructive Editing:[/B] Switch between [B]Visual Mode[/B] and [B]Raw Text[/B] without losing your custom parameters or comments.
[*] [B]Error Handling:[/B] Improved parsing prevents SDEF corruption from trailing comments or special characters.
[/LIST]

[HR][/HR]
